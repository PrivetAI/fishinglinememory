# FishingLineMemory

iOS app for anglers to track fishing line condition across multiple rods.

## App Store

**Subtitle:** Track Line Health & Get Smart Alerts

**Description:**
Keep your fishing lines in peak condition with Line Memory — the essential app for serious anglers.

Track the health of your fishing lines across all your rods. Know exactly when it's time to replace based on both age and actual usage. Never lose a fish to worn-out line again.

**Key Features:**
• Rod Management — Add all your rods with line type, diameter, and change dates
• Health Tracking — Visual indicators show line condition at a glance
• Smart Reminders — Get alerts based on days AND fishing trips
• Trip Logging — Record your fishing sessions and track line usage
• Statistics — See your fishing activity and line change history
• Spool Calculator — Calculate exactly how much line you need for any reel
• Knots Guide — Learn 8 essential fishing knots with step-by-step instructions
• Line Comparison — Compare monofilament, braided, fluorocarbon, and hybrid lines

Works completely offline. Your data stays on your device.

**Promotional Text:**
Track your fishing line health, get smart replacement reminders, and access essential fishing tools — all in one app!
